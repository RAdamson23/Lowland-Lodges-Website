Room Types


We're proud to present the three types of rooms that are available for booking.
Our rooms cater to a wide variety of needs and will suit a range of customers.


One Room Lodge

Our single room lodge provides great accommodation for upto two people. This cozy lodge comes included with a kitchen, one queen size bed and a 40 inch TV (with sky included).

Two Room Lodge

This two room lodge provides great accommodation for a family. This spacious lodge comes included with a kitchen, one queen size bed and three single beds. A 40 inch TV (with sky) is also included.

Studio

Our studio presents a clean, spacious and professonal interior for you and your colleagues. Fitted with a 10 person conference table, overhead projector and air conditioning, this studio will make your company meetings much more enjoyable.
